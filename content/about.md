I'm Blake Merryman, a software developer based in Colorado. I've been writing software for Apple platforms since 2014 — and tinkering as a hobbyist long before that.

I spent seven and a half years at Apple and worked on a lot of really cool features, namely sleep experiences for [Health](https://www.apple.com/health/) and system experiences for [Apple Vision Pro](https://www.apple.com/os/visionos/).

At the end of 2025, I left to build something of my own. Merryman Technology is where that happens.

Apple devices were the first computers I really felt at home on. I love building experiences that are delightful and solve real problems. I hope you enjoy what I bring into the world.
