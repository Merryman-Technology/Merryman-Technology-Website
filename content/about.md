Merryman Technology was founded by [Blake Merryman](https://www.blakemerryman.com/about/) in 2026 as an independent app development studio with a singular focus: building quality software for Apple platforms.

Blake spent seven and a half years at Apple — contributing to sleep features in the Health app and the Guest User experience for Apple Vision Pro.

Good software should spark a little joy. We're working on it.
